## If You Still See Builder.io Logo

This is likely **browser cache**. Clear it:

### Chrome/Edge
1. Press `Cmd + Shift + Delete` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + Delete` (Windows)
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Click "Clear data"
4. Refresh the page with `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows)

### Safari
1. Go to Safari → Settings → Privacy
2. Click "Manage Website Data"
3. Remove localhost
4. Refresh with `Cmd + Option + R`

### Firefox
1. Press `Cmd + Shift + Delete` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + Delete` (Windows)
2. Select "Cache"
3. Click "Clear Now"
4. Refresh with `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows)

## Or Use Incognito/Private Mode

Open the app in incognito/private browsing mode to see it without cache:
- Chrome: `Cmd + Shift + N` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + N` (Windows)
- Safari: `Cmd + Shift + N`
- Firefox: `Cmd + Shift + P` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + P` (Windows)

Then visit: http://localhost:3000
